How to use Style Mi
  1. To get started, Fill out the form, and press start to begin.

  2. Click on the Create Look icon to choose the ideal weather and style for your occasion.

  3. Once you selected your prefered weather and style, your outfit will appear on your avatar. You can then click the arrows to the right and left to see variations.

  4. Once you've completed your look, click on Add to Closet to save it or click on the Reset button to return to the create look options.

  5. Click on the closet icon to view passed saved looks or delete previously saved looks.
