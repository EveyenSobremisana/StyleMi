How to use Style Mi
  1. To get started click Begin and fill out the form. Press start to be directed to the Avatar.

  2. Click on the Change Look icon to choose the ideal weather and style for your occasion.

  3. Once you selected your prefered weather and style, your outfit will appear on your avatar. You can then click the arrows to the right and left to see variations. Click on the Information Icon to display facts.

  4. Once you've completed your look, click on Add to Closet button in the top right of your screen to save it

  5. Click on the closet icon to view or to delete your saved outfit. If you click on View Mi, you will be redirected to your final look.

  6. Click on the back button to return to the close!

  7. Enjoy Style Mi !
Style Mi is an event based planning app designed to make the everyday life of both men and women just a little bit easier when it comes to deciding on what to wear. Style Mi brings the fun back into fashion planning and makes it convenient for users to decide on possible outfits ahead of time.

At Style Mi every result is based on user inputs to help guide you to your ideal outfit. Style Mi will not only save you time but will also help you discover new fashion trends you may never have thought of trying before. Our clothing designs have a modern, fashion forward feel that you are sure to love. Get started now and discover the best new looks with Style Mi!
Meet the creators of Style Mi, Keara and Eveyen ! We are both BCIT students in the Digital Design and Development program and we wanted to create a fun and interactive app with a focus on convenience and fashion.

That is how we came up with Style Mi! We have combined our UX and UI design capabilities to come up with the ideal app for both men and women looking for a bit of help when it comes to deciding and planning what to wear. We hope you like it!
